About Midshires Heritage Railway

Welcome to Midshires Heritage Railway, where we preserve and celebrate the history of Britain’s railways. Our railway is run by a passionate team of volunteers dedicated to restoring historic locomotives, carriages, and stations for future generations to enjoy.

Located in the heart of the countryside, Midshires Heritage Railway offers visitors the chance to experience the golden age of steam travel. From relaxing journeys through scenic landscapes to special events and family days out, there is something for everyone to enjoy.

Our mission is to keep railway heritage alive through restoration projects, education, and unforgettable experiences. Whether you are a railway enthusiast or visiting for the first time, we look forward to welcoming you aboard.
